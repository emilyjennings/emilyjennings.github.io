Web Development Projects

Jaime Andrews Fitness Training

January 2018

My first freelance project was this website. Jaime Andrews needed a simple wordpress site for her health coaching clients to see what she offers. I was proud to help her start fresh and create a thriving business. She provided me with some design direction and images/content, and I used Wordpress for the first time to customize a pre-made theme. I used only HTML & CSS here because that was all she required, though I am capable of more. I learned how to make a CSS hamburger navigation menu in order to spice it up a bit. This took me about 20 hours total to complete, including contact time with GoDaddy to make a subdomain and then make the site live.
