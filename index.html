Hi! I like programming. A lot. This is it. I am meant to code. I recently only feel truly myself when I am coding. This is my poetry that turns into ways to change the world. Welcome to my happy place - it’s evolving all the time ad I pick up new skills. I want to meet you and see how you can change my world! Teach me new technologies because I am forever growing. I started making this site in November 2017 as my first-ever web development project, and I have been adding to it ever since. Enjoy and reach out!

Empathy

I believe that empathy is the key to our success as a community. The human race cannot truly progress and overcome barriers unless we can step into each others' shoes.

Authenticity

Authenticity is so important to me throughout the realms of life. When you are honest and bring your true self to a situation, the group can work toward more holistic solutions.

Growth

Learning and tackling new problems expands the mind and opens up doors that weren't visible before. I am a big fan of solving seemingly insurmountable problems! I love it when people approach problems in novel ways.

Diversity

In Korea, for 5 years, I was a western face in a crowd of Korean faces. At times I craved the wildly different points of view I grew up with. Creating communities where diverse ideas are sought and encouraged is so important.

What I do

I am helping others realize their potential and exploring new ways of thinking while creating tools to make the world a better place.

What I am seeking

I am hoping to solve meaningful problems together as a community and for a community. I’d love to learn skills outside my paradigm while doing it!
