Hello world, I've been practicing my tech skills while creating this website. I started by making the basic styling with HTML and CSS, spending hours choosing the color themes I wanted which represent a Jeju beach on a cold day. Then, I learned JavaScript and jQuery and made some interactive features. I was lucky to have a great teacher. I am discovering that I love coding, and I may be slightly addicted because I really enjoy puzzles! You'll see this website evolve over time for sure.

Please explore this site to see the things I love about life. I'd love to find out your story!

Empathy

I believe that empathy is the key to our success as a community. The human race cannot truly progress and overcome barriers unless we can step into each others' shoes.

Authenticity

Authenticity is so important to me throughout the realms of life. When you are honest and bring your true self to a situation, the team can work toward more holistic solutions.

Growth

Learning and tackling new problems expands the brain and opens up doors that weren't visible before. I am a big fan of solving seemingly insurmountable problems! I love it when people approach problems in novel ways. My goal is to be learning continuously and to keep up with the changing world.

Diversity

In Korea, for 5 years, I was a western face in a crowd of Korean faces. At times I craved the diversity and wildly different points of view that living in America brought me. Creating communities where different backgrounds and diverse ideas are sought and encouraged is something I support strongly.

What I do

I am connecting people in my community to each other and helping identify the ways we can really change the world, and I am just getting started!
