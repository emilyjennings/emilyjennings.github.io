Empathy

I believe that empathy is the key to our success as a community. The human race cannot truly progress and overcome barriers unless we can step into each others' shoes.

Authenticity

Authenticity is so important to me throughout the realms of life. When you are honest and bring your true self to a situation, the team can work toward more holistic solutions.

Growth

Learning and tackling new problems expands the brain and opens up doors that weren't visible before. I am a big fan of solving seemingly insurmountable problems! I love it when people approach problems in novel ways. My goal is to be learning continuously and to keep up with the changing world.

Diversity

In Korea, for 5 years, I was a western face in a crowd of Korean faces. At times I craved the diversity and wildly different points of view that living in America brought me growing up in PG County, Maryland. Creating communities where different backgrounds and diverse ideas are sought and encouraged is something I support strongly.

What I do

I am connecting people in my community to each other and helping identify the ways we can really change the world, and I am just getting started!

In my life I seem to often find myself helping people thrive and guiding them through journeys of various levels of transformation. This is why I love working in education, but I know that this is a part of so many other fields of interesting work. I love people and I am inspired by the strengths of humanity. I am truly alive when I am connecting to people around me in meaningful ways. I enjoy situations where my patience is put to the test and I can discover my own abilities to solve problems in the moment.
